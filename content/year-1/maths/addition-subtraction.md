# Addition and Subtraction — Year 1 Maths

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn how to **add** and **subtract** numbers up to 20. You will use **number bonds** and the **part-whole model** to help you.

## Key Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| add | put numbers together to find the total |
| subtract | take away a number to find what is left |
| number bond | two numbers that go together to make another (e.g. 3 + 7 = 10) |
| part-whole | a diagram showing a whole split into two parts |
| equals (=) | the answer is |

## Let's Explore

### Adding
🍎🍎🍎 + 🍎🍎 = 🍎🍎🍎🍎🍎
**3 + 2 = 5**

### Subtracting
🍪🍪🍪🍪🍪 – 🍪🍪 = 🍪🍪🍪
**5 – 2 = 3**

### Number Bonds to 10
```
0 + 10 = 10
1 +  9 = 10
2 +  8 = 10
3 +  7 = 10
4 +  6 = 10
5 +  5 = 10
```

### Part-Whole Model
```
    [10]
   /    \
 [4]   [6]
```
4 and 6 are **parts**. 10 is the **whole**.
