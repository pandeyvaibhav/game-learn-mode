# Plants — Year 1 Science

## Big Question
What are the parts of a plant, and what does a plant need to grow?

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn the names of the main **parts of a plant** and what each part does. You will also discover what plants need to grow healthy and strong.

## Key Science Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| root | the part underground that takes in water |
| stem | holds the plant up and carries water to the leaves |
| leaf | makes food for the plant using sunlight |
| flower | the colourful part that helps the plant make seeds |
| seed | a tiny package that can grow into a new plant |
| sunlight | light from the sun that plants need to make food |

## The Science Explained

### Parts of a Plant
```
     🌸  ← Flower
      |
    🍃🍃 ← Leaves
      |
      |   ← Stem
    ──┼──
    //|\\  ← Roots
```

| Part | Job |
|---|---|
| Roots | hold the plant in the soil and soak up water |
| Stem | supports the plant and carries water up from the roots |
| Leaves | catch sunlight and make food for the plant |
| Flower | attracts insects and makes seeds for new plants |

### What Plants Need to Grow
1. **Water** — roots drink it from the soil
2. **Sunlight** — leaves use it to make food
3. **Air** — leaves take in air
4. **Soil** — gives the roots a place to grow and provides nutrients

If any of these are missing, the plant will not grow well.

## Quick Quiz
1. Which part of the plant is underground? → *Roots*
2. What do leaves use to make food? → *Sunlight*
3. Name two things a plant needs to grow. → *Water and sunlight (also air and soil)*
4. What is the job of the stem? → *To hold the plant up and carry water to the leaves*

## Science in the Real World
Gardeners make sure plants get enough water and sunlight. Farmers grow crops like wheat and potatoes by looking after the soil. Even the fruit we eat comes from the flower part of a plant!
