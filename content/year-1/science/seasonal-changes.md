# Seasonal Changes вАФ Year 1 Science

## What We're Learning
You are going to learn about the four seasons and how the weather and daylight change throughout the year.

## Key Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| Season | A time of year with its own weather pattern |
| Spring | The season after winter when things start to grow |
| Summer | The warmest season with the longest days |
| Autumn | The season when leaves fall and days get shorter |
| Winter | The coldest season with the shortest days |
| Daylight | The hours of light during the day |

## Let's Explore

### The four seasons

```
рЯМЄ Spring вЖТ вШАпЄП Summer вЖТ рЯНВ Autumn вЖТ вЭДпЄП Winter вЖТ рЯМЄ Spring ...
```

The seasons repeat every year in the same order.

### What happens in each season?

| Season | Weather | Nature | Daylight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Spring рЯМЄ | Getting warmer, showers | Flowers bloom, baby animals born | Getting longer |
| Summer вШАпЄП | Warm and sunny | Trees full of green leaves | Longest days |
| Autumn рЯНВ | Getting cooler, windy | Leaves change colour and fall | Getting shorter |
| Winter вЭДпЄП | Cold, frost, snow | Trees bare, animals hibernate | Shortest days |

### Daylight hours
In summer, the sun rises early and sets late вАФ there is lots of daylight.
In winter, the sun rises late and sets early вАФ there is less daylight.

```
Summer:  вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП (long day)
Winter:  вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП вШАпЄП (short day)
```

### Example вАФ What season is it?
The leaves are falling off the trees and it is getting windy.
It is **autumn**.

## Did You Know? рЯФђ
Some animals sleep through the whole winter! This is called hibernation. Hedgehogs, dormice and some bats all hibernate to save energy when it is cold and food is hard to find.
