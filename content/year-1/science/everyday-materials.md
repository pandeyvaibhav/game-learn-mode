# Everyday Materials — Year 1 Science

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn about **materials** — what things are made of. You will describe materials using words like **hard**, **soft**, **rough** and **smooth**.

## Key Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| material | what something is made from |
| wood | a material from trees |
| plastic | a light, man-made material |
| metal | a hard, shiny material |
| glass | a see-through material |
| property | what a material is like (hard, soft, smooth) |

## Let's Explore

### Common Materials
| Material | Properties | Example Object |
|---|---|---|
| Wood 🪵 | hard, strong, rough | table, pencil |
| Plastic 🧴 | light, waterproof, smooth | water bottle, toy |
| Metal 🔩 | hard, shiny, strong | spoon, coin |
| Glass 🪟 | see-through, smooth, breakable | window, jar |
| Fabric 👕 | soft, flexible, light | clothes, curtains |

### Describing Properties
- **Hard** or **soft**?
- **Rough** or **smooth**?
- **Shiny** or **dull**?
- **Bendy** or **stiff**?
- **Waterproof** or **absorbent**?

## Did You Know?
Glass is made by heating sand to a very high temperature — over 1,700°C! The sand melts and becomes a clear liquid that cools into glass.
