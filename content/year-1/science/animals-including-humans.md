# Animals Including Humans — Year 1 Science

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn about your **body parts**, your **five senses**, and what animals (including humans) need to **stay alive**.

## Key Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| senses | the five ways we learn about the world |
| sight | seeing with our eyes |
| hearing | listening with our ears |
| smell | using our nose to detect smells |
| taste | using our tongue to notice flavours |
| touch | feeling with our skin |

## Let's Explore

### The Five Senses
| Sense | Body Part | We use it to… |
|---|---|---|
| Sight 👀 | Eyes | see colours, shapes, movement |
| Hearing 👂 | Ears | hear sounds and voices |
| Smell 👃 | Nose | smell flowers, food, rain |
| Taste 👅 | Tongue | taste sweet, salty, sour, bitter |
| Touch ✋ | Skin | feel hot, cold, soft, rough |

### What Do Animals Need?
All animals need:
- 🥤 **Water** to drink
- 🍎 **Food** to eat
- 💨 **Air** to breathe
- 🏠 **Shelter** to stay safe

### Body Parts
Head, shoulders, knees and toes — plus arms, legs, hands, feet, eyes, ears, nose and mouth!

## Did You Know?
Dogs can hear sounds that are too high for humans to hear. They can also smell things that are 100,000 times weaker than what our noses can detect!
