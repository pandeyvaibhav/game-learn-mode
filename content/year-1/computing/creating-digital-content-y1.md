# Creating Digital Content — Year 1 Computing

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn how to create things on a **computer** or **tablet**. You will practise using the **keyboard** and **mouse** and learn how to **save** your work.

## Key Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| digital | made on a computer or tablet |
| keyboard | the buttons with letters and numbers |
| mouse | the device that moves the pointer on screen |
| save | keep your work so you can find it later |
| file | a piece of saved work on a computer |
| undo | go back and remove your last action |

## Let's Explore

### What Can You Create?
- 🎨 **Pictures** using a paint program
- ✏️ **Words** using a word processor
- 🖼️ **Slideshows** with pictures and text

### Using a Keyboard
| Key | What it does |
|---|---|
| Space bar | adds a gap between words |
| Enter | starts a new line |
| Backspace | deletes the last letter |
| Shift | makes a capital letter |

### Using a Mouse
- **Click** — press the left button
- **Drag** — hold the button and move
- **Double click** — click twice quickly to open something

### Saving Your Work
Always save your work so you do not lose it! Look for the 💾 save button or press Ctrl + S.

## Did You Know?
The first computer mouse was made of wood! It was invented by Douglas Engelbart in 1964. It had only one button and a wooden shell.
