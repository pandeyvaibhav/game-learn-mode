# Algorithms and Instructions — Year 1 Computing

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn what an **algorithm** is — a set of step-by-step instructions. You will practise putting steps in the **right order** and learn about **debugging**.

## Key Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| algorithm | a set of steps to complete a task |
| instruction | telling someone what to do |
| sequence | steps in the correct order |
| debug | find and fix a mistake |
| program | instructions that a computer follows |

## Let's Explore

### What is an Algorithm?
An algorithm is like a **recipe**. If you follow the steps in order, you get the right result!

**Algorithm: Brushing Your Teeth**
1. Pick up your toothbrush
2. Put toothpaste on the brush
3. Brush your teeth for two minutes
4. Rinse your mouth with water
5. Put the toothbrush away

### Order Matters!
What if you did step 3 before step 2? You would brush with no toothpaste! 😄

### Debugging
If something goes wrong, you **debug** — find the mistake and fix it.

❌ Wrong order: "Put on shoes → Put on socks"
✅ Fixed: "Put on socks → Put on shoes"
