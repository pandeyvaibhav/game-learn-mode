# Biographies — Year 5 English

## Big Question
What makes a good biography and how is it different from an autobiography?

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn the features of a **biography**: writing about someone else's life in **chronological order**, using **third person** and **formal language**.

## Key English Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| biography | the story of someone's life written by another person |
| autobiography | the story of someone's life written by themselves |
| chronological | in time order (earliest to latest) |
| third person | using he/she/they (not I/me) |
| formal language | serious, professional tone |
| legacy | what someone is remembered for |

## The English Explained

### Biography vs Autobiography
| Biography | Autobiography |
|---|---|
| Written by someone else | Written by the person themselves |
| Uses third person (he/she) | Uses first person (I/me) |
| Researched from sources | Based on personal memory |

### Structure
1. **Introduction** — who the person is and why they are important.
2. **Early life** — childhood, family, education.
3. **Key achievements** — what they did that was significant.
4. **Later life / legacy** — what happened afterwards and how they are remembered.

### Language Features
- **Third person**: *She was born in…*
- **Past tense**: *He discovered…*
- **Time connectives**: *After that… Later… By 1960…*
- **Formal tone**: *She is widely regarded as…*

### Example Opening
> **Mary Anning** was born in 1799 in Lyme Regis, Dorset. She would go on to become one of the most important fossil hunters in history.

## Quick Quiz
1. What is a biography? → *The story of someone's life written by another person*
2. What person is a biography written in? → *Third person (he/she/they)*
3. What does chronological mean? → *In time order*
4. Name one difference between a biography and autobiography. → *Biography is written by someone else; autobiography by the person themselves*

## English in the Real World
Biographies are published as books, films, and documentaries. Historians write biographies to preserve important lives. Wikipedia entries are a form of mini biography.

## Learning Checklist
- [ ] I know the difference between biography and autobiography
- [ ] I can write in chronological order using third person
- [ ] I can use formal language and time connectives
