# Online Safety 🎯

## What We're Learning
We are learning how to stay safe when we use the internet. The internet is a wonderful tool for learning and having fun, but we need to know some important rules to keep ourselves safe online.

## Key Words

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| personal information | private details about you, like your name, address or school |
| password | a secret word or phrase that keeps your account safe |
| trusted adult | a grown-up you know and trust, like a parent, carer or teacher |
| online | connected to the internet |

## Let's Explore! 🔍

### Keep Personal Information Private
**Personal information** is anything that could help someone find out who you are or where you live. You should **never** share these things online with people you don't know:

- Your full name
- Your address
- Your school name
- Your phone number
- Your age or birthday
- Photos of yourself

If a website or game asks for your personal information, **always ask a trusted adult** before typing anything.

### Passwords
A password is like a key to your online accounts. Good password rules:

- **Keep it secret** — don't tell your friends (only a parent or carer)
- **Make it hard to guess** — don't use your name or "1234"
- **Don't use the same password** for everything

### Being Kind Online
When we are online, we should behave the same way we would in real life:

- **Be kind** — don't write mean things to others
- **Be respectful** — treat others how you want to be treated
- **Think before you post** — once something is online, it's hard to take back

If someone is unkind to you online, **tell a trusted adult** straight away.

### What To Do If Something Worries You
If you ever see something online that makes you feel:
- Scared
- Upset
- Confused
- Uncomfortable

**Tell a trusted adult immediately.** You will never get in trouble for telling someone.

Remember the rule: **If in doubt, tell a trusted adult!**
