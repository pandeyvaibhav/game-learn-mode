# Grammar Word Classes — Year 2 English

## Big Question
What types of words do we use when we write?

## What We're Learning
In this lesson you will learn about four important **word classes**: **nouns**, **verbs**, **adjectives**, and **adverbs**. Knowing word classes helps you build interesting sentences.

## Key English Words
| Word | What it means |
|---|---|
| noun | a naming word — a person, place, or thing |
| verb | a doing or being word — run, jump, is |
| adjective | a describing word — big, red, fluffy |
| adverb | tells us how something is done — quickly, carefully |
| word class | a group that a word belongs to |
| sentence | a group of words that makes complete sense |

## The English Explained

### Nouns (naming words)
Nouns name **people**, **places**, and **things**:
- People: *teacher*, *mum*, *Sam*
- Places: *school*, *park*, *London*
- Things: *ball*, *table*, *cat*

### Verbs (doing / being words)
Verbs tell us what someone **does** or **is**:
- *The dog **runs** in the garden.*
- *She **is** happy.*

### Adjectives (describing words)
Adjectives describe nouns:
- *The **fluffy** cat sat on the **soft** mat.*
- *He wore a **red** hat.*

### Adverbs (how words)
Adverbs describe how a verb is done — they often end in **-ly**:
- *She sang **quietly**.*
- *The boy ran **quickly**.*

### Putting It Together
> *The **tiny** mouse **crept** **silently** across the **wooden** floor.*

| Word | Class |
|---|---|
| tiny | adjective |
| mouse | noun |
| crept | verb |
| silently | adverb |
| wooden | adjective |
| floor | noun |

## Quick Quiz
1. What word class is "dog"? → *Noun*
2. What word class is "jumped"? → *Verb*
3. What word class is "happy"? → *Adjective*
4. What word class is "slowly"? → *Adverb*

## English in the Real World
Every sentence you read or write uses word classes. Poets pick adjectives and adverbs carefully to paint pictures with words!

## Learning Checklist
- [ ] I can identify nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs
- [ ] I can use adjectives and adverbs to make sentences more interesting
- [ ] I can sort words into their word classes
