# Forces and Magnets - Year 3 Science

Pushes and pulls are **forces**. They help objects start moving, slow down, stop, or change direction.

Magnets are a special force that can pull certain metals without touching them. Magnets attract iron and steel, not all metals, and every magnet has a **north pole** and a **south pole**.
