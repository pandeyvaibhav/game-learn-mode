# How a Fossil Forms — Year 3 Science

**Fossils are the remains or shapes of living things that are trapped inside rock.** They can be millions of years old!

A fossil forms when an animal or plant dies and is quickly **buried** by mud or sand. Over a very long time, more and more layers press down, the soft parts disappear, and the hard shape stays behind in the rock. Much later, people dig it up.
