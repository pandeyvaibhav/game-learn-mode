# What's in Soil? — Year 3 Science

**Soil is what plants grow in.** It looks like just brown dirt, but it's actually a mixture of four things.

Soil has **rock bits** (from rocks breaking down over a long time), **rotted plants** (leaves and twigs that have broken down), **water**, and **air**. Plants need all four to grow well!
