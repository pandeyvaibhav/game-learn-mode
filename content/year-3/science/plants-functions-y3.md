# Parts of a Plant — Year 3 Science

Every plant has four main parts, and each one has a job. The **roots** drink water from the soil. The **stem** holds the plant up and carries water to the leaves. The **leaves** catch sunlight to make food. The **flower** makes seeds so new plants can grow.

The **soil** is not part of the plant, but plants need it — it holds water and tiny minerals the roots drink up.
