# Rocks and Fossils - Year 3 Science

There are three main rock types: **igneous**, **sedimentary**, and **metamorphic**. They form in different ways over long periods of time.

Igneous rock forms when lava cools, sedimentary rock forms in layers, and metamorphic rock is changed by heat and pressure. Fossils are usually found in sedimentary rock because layers can trap and protect remains.
