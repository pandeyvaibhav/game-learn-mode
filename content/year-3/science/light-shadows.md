# Light and Shadows - Year 3 Science

Light travels in straight lines from a source such as the Sun, a lamp, or a torch. We can only see when light reaches our eyes.

Shadows form when an opaque object blocks light from reaching a surface. Transparent materials such as glass let most light through, so they make little or no shadow. Translucent materials let some light through.
